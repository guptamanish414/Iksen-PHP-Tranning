Online YearBook

This site is developed based on the idea of friendship and is a small tribute to our college life.
4 long years have passed and here we are at the verge of concluding our college life.
In these years, all of us have grown together - we have battled together... we have fallen together... and we have rejoiced together.
Together we experienced the joys of college life....by playing pranks, by sharing secrets and by sharing jokes.
Not only joys but also the fears of college life....Exams, no preparation, carrying cheats and the announcement of results.
Years may pass but sweet and sour memories collected over time will never fade and these are what we are going to cherish rest of our lives.
We are going to miss our college life but not our friends....
